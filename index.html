Membership Benefits

The exclusive membership program at OGMA offers unique benefits and privileges to its members. Token holders can use their tokens to access OGMA's state-of-the-art fleet of super yachts, all included in the yearly fee. This allows members to experience a luxury yacht experience that is not available to the public.

The membership program at OGMA is designed to be exclusive and selective. The number of memberships is limited, which ensures that the club remains exclusive and provides an unmatched experience to its members. To become a member, individuals must hold a certain number of OGMA tokens. Once a member, they can enjoy all the privileges that come with the membership, including access to the exclusive yacht club and its fleet of super yachts.

Overall, OGMA offers a unique and exclusive yacht club experience that is powered by blockchain technology. With its innovative platform, members can enjoy a seamless and secure booking process, and access to an unparalleled fleet of luxury yachts.
